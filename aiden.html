    About

    Aiden is an ontology management tool and framework for building intelligent systems, specifically designed for use with Product Data; a sharp editorial tool that makes manual product data manipulation smooth. Battle tested with thousands of categories, over 30 attributes per category and more than 1M products classified, normalized and delivered using the tool, it is a perfect playground for your taxonomy and schema before you commit your ontology into your Master Data Management system.
